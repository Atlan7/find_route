About site

Hello, welcome to my pet project "Find Route", on this site you can find necessary (not real) route from one city to another by train for a certain period. Besides, you can watch cities and trains schedule. If the city has routes you can watch them by click view trains from city or to city.

Also you can create an account. If you forget your password you can reset him though your email.
